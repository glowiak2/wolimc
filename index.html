WoliMC


WoliMC is a launcher for the game Minecraft free of political content.
No rainbows and other political things. It also has support for instances and [UPCOMMING] mods

Aside that, WoliMC is written in Java and runs on every platform.
Please have on mind that it's yet in very early stage and may not work properly.
It does not yet have Mojang/Microsoft authentication, so no chance to connect to servers like Hypixel at the moment.
If you want to download this monster anyway, see the bottom of this page.
